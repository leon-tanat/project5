Top Three Recent SNL Skits

Saturday Night Live (SNL) is an American skit variety and comedy television show that has been on for decades and airs on NBC. It began in 1975 and features celebrity guest hosts, musical performances, and satirical skits parodies of politics, pop culture, and the current events. Listed below are my top three favorite SNL skits that I think you will also love.

Ranking...


Saturday Night Live: Protective Mom, 2023 YouTube

#1 Protective Mom

I love this skit because it humorously exaggerates the overprotectiveness of a mother greeting her child's new boyfriend, making it both authentic and absurd. It is a little cringe and over the top, which is very entertaining. The acting and writing is really great with a humorous twist in the end.


Saturday Night Live: The Iceberg On the Sinking of the Titanic, 2021 YouTube

#2 The Iceberg On the Sinking of the Titanic

Here Bowen Yang in the role of the iceberg as an underdog instead of a villain. The ridiculous premise of the skits coupled with acerbic satire and straight-man acting are what are so laugh-out-loud hilarious. Satirizing a tragedy like this in such a manner creates a fresh and current take on things that never grows stale and keeps people chuckling.


Saturday Night Live: Rap Roundtable, 2020 YouTube

#3 Rap Roundtables

This skit laughably juxtaposes old-school and new-school rap perspectives, satirizing generation arguments surrounding hip-hop. The sketch's fusion of serious music critique and absurd characters makes it hilarious as well as charming. With amazing performances by Pete Davidson and Timothee Chalamet and excellent writing, the humor is enhanced, making the skit real funny and awesome.
